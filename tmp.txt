<p>
<b>Locust bean gum</b> (<b>LBG</b>, also known as <b>carob gum</b>,
<b>carob bean gum</b>, <b>carobin</b>,
<b><a href="/wiki/E_number" title="E number">E410</a></b>) is a
<a href="/wiki/Galactomannan" title="Galactomannan">galactomannan</a>
<a href="/wiki/Gum_(botany)" title="Gum (botany)">vegetable gum</a>
extracted from the <a href="/wiki/Seed" title="Seed">seeds</a> of the
<a href="/wiki/Ceratonia_siliqua" class="mw-redirect" title="Ceratonia siliqua">carob
tree</a> and used as a
<a href="/wiki/Thickening_agent" title="Thickening agent">thickening
agent</a> (gelling agent) in
<a href="/wiki/Food_technology" title="Food technology">food
technology</a>.
